About Us

We specialize in fast and secure IMAP email and WordPress website hosting with free support for simple problems.

You can purchase a new domain name through us or transfer your current domain and we'll manage your DNS (Domain Name System), website and mail services for your online personal or business presence.

Our servers are located in a secure Sydney data centre with multiple gigabit IP transit and peering links and hosted on high grade DELL hardware with mirrored local and offsite backup storage drives.

Although we primarily support Linux Desktop users, our services will work with any standard desktop, tablet or mobile phone device using any modern browser or dedicated email program.

We encourage the use of free and open source Linux Desktop systems like Kubuntu and applications such as Firefox for web browsing and Thunderbird as an IMAP email program all of which we fully support.

General Notes

We do not provide POP email services because it does not work with our server side spam filter or shared mailboxes from multiple devices.

Wordpress is pre-installed with a TwentySeventeen Child-Theme ready for you to customize and is auto updated once a week.

Shared hosting plans include unmetered bandwidth for web and mail traffic but abusive traffic trends may lead to suspended services.

All our servers are mirrored every night to our offsite backup servers so all files can be restored from the previous day upon request.

Long term snapshot backups are the responsibility of the client however we are more than happy to help facilitate your backup procedure via SFTP, Rsync or even NFS if you have a static IP. Just submit a support ticket and we will provide specific instructions for your particular situation.

Monthly and annual prices are in AUD (Australian dollars) and includes 10% GST tax for Australian citizens. USD pricing is available in the shopping cart. If you have any billing or account issues then feel free to submit a billing ticket so we can assist you.
