About RentaNet

We provide domain, email and web hosting services primarily for linux desktop users

Our aim is to provide cost effective and secure hosting for Wordpress websites and IMAP Email services for your personal or business Domain. You can purchase a new domain name through us or transfer your current domain and we'll manage your DNS (Domain Name System), website and mail services for you. Most of our clients use Wordpress as their web solution of choice which we fully support with automatic updates and free help and advice for simple problems. We specialize in using the latest Ubuntu Server Linux OS on our servers with nginx and PHP7 FPM for fast and efficient web hosting. SMTP and IMAP email services are handled by Postfix and Dovecot and MariaDB is our backend database system. LetsEncrypt provides free and auto-updated SSL certificates for both mail and web security. Our servers are located in a secure Sydney data centre with multiple gigabit IP transit and peering links and hosted on high grade DELL hardware with mirrored local and offsite storage drives.

General Notes

We do not provide POP email services because it does not work with our custom server side spam filtering system and, in general, does not play well with multiple devices and shared mailboxes.

Wordpress is pre-installed with a TwentySeventeen Child-Theme ready for you to customize and is auto updated once a week.

All shared hosting plans include unmetered bandwidth for normal web and mail traffic but abusive traffic trends will get our attention and may lead to suspended services.

We do not provide incremental long term backups, instead, our servers are mirrored via Rsync every night to offsite out-of-AU servers in Singapore and the US. All files can be restored from the previous day upon request. Long term snapshot backups are the responsibility of the client however we are more than happy to help facilitate your backup procedure via SFTP, Rsync or even NFS if you have a static IP. Just submit a support ticket and we will provide specific instructions for your particular situation.

Default monthly and annual prices are in AUD (Australian dollars) and includes 10% GST tax for Australian citizens. USD pricing is available in the shopping cart. If you have any billing or account issues then feel free to submit a billing ticket so we can assist you.
